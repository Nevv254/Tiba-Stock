Our Mission

TibaStock aims to eliminate preventable deaths and treatment delays in Kenyan healthcare facilities by enabling real-time tracking and sharing of essential medical stock data.

Why TibaStock Was Created

In Kenya, rural clinics and even urban hospitals often suffer from unpredictable stock-outs of vital medicines, forcing patients to travel long distances or go without treatment. TibaStock was built to give healthcare workers better visibility and control over their inventory.

Who We Serve

Our solution supports frontline health workers across clinics, hospitals, pharmacies, and county public health offices, empowering them to make timely and life-saving decisions.
